### The Problem
The National Bank of Rwanda oversees the licensing of every commercial bank and financial institution
operating in the country. Today this process is entirely manual ,applications arrive by email, documents
are tracked in spreadsheets, and approval decisions pass through informal channels. There is no single
source of truth, no reliable audit trail, and no visibility into where any application stands at a given
moment.You have been asked to design and build a Bank Licensing & Compliance Portal to replace this
process end to end.

You are not being given a specification. You are being given a problem. What you build, how you
structure it, what you prioritise, and what you deliberately leave out , and the reasoning behind every
one of those decisions, is what this assessment is about. There are intentional gaps in these
requirements. How you handle ambiguity whether you surface it, make a defensible call, and document
it is part of what we are evaluating.
