## Design Document
Include a design document in your repository. It must cover:
- Your architecture :components, how they communicate, why you structured it this way
- Your data model : schema for applications, users, documents and audit log
- Your state machine : every state, every valid transition, and the rules that govern them
- Your roles : every role, what they can and cannot do and why you drew the lines where you did
- The hard decisions : for each non-negotiable requirement above, explain specifically how your
implementation satisfies it and what you would do differently or add given more time

NB :A design document that shows clear thinking on trade-offs carries more weight than one that simply
describes what was built.
